# Window Timeline

## Context Turns 1-10

- Turn 1: The story opens with Paz remembering Mint's fatal wound, then waking during the Revival Day festival in New Sicily. Karina returns Paz's press card. Paz has an old Seagull 4B film camera, a group photo, and an anonymous photo showing the supposedly dead Mint in New Sicily.
- Turn 2: Paz asks the vendor about Karina. The vendor says Karina is not mafia but is recognized across the streets, and warns Paz to keep distance.
- Turn 3: Paz says he will start searching in Dark Street.
- Turn 4: Paz suggests the vendor is not ordinary. She answers that people who survive in Dark Street rarely look like their jobs.
- Turn 5: The vendor says Paz looks like someone looking for dead people and asks whether he is seeking a person or a place for atonement.
- Turn 6: Paz says he only wants to find the person in the photo. The vendor warns that finding someone in New Sicily and proving you are not here to die can become the same thing.
- Turn 7: Paz leaves the festival and walks into Dark Street, with the press card in his pocket and the camera against his ribs.
- Turn 8: Paz searches the street, noticing scraped wall marks and old shoeprints leading toward a narrow alley.
- Turn 9: Paz examines the shoeprints and infers at least two people passed through the alley after stopping near the scraped wall.
- Turn 10: Paz follows the shoeprints into the alley and finds a partially open green iron door with fresh marks around the lock.

## Evaluated Turns 11-20

- Turn 11: Paz pushes through the iron door into a dark underground space. The text briefly says cold metal is felt through gloves, then immediately corrects that Paz is not wearing gloves. Choices include using a camera flash to light the room.
- Turn 12: Paz approaches a metallic reflection and finds a scraped identification plate by a rotten wooden box.
- Turn 13: Paz studies the plate and concludes its original identifying text was deliberately scraped away.
- Turn 14: Paz examines the wooden box shipping mark, but the remaining text is too faded to read.
- Turn 15: Paz checks other boxes. Only the box with the removed plate appears deliberately targeted. He finds a small plastic fragment and senses faint airflow deeper inside.
- Turn 16: Paz follows the airflow along a wall into a deeper passage. A choice again offers using the camera flash.
- Turn 17: Paz reaches another iron door, detects colder air, smoke smell, movement, and metal sounds beyond it.
- Turn 18: Paz enters the next space, where weak light and a warmer artificial light source suggest someone is present.
- Turn 19: Paz moves toward the artificial light and waits behind paper boxes, listening to movement and metal being set down.
- Turn 20: Paz peeks around the boxes and sees a red-haired woman arranging bandages and ammunition under a work light. He recognizes her as Mint, though she has changed and has a new scar.
