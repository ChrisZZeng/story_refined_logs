# Window Timeline 11-30

## 11-20 Context

- Turn 11: The player circles the clock tower and finds a narrow side angle on the alley entrance. At the wall base, they discover three copper-red hairs protected in a recess, strongly implying Mint stood there.
- Turn 12: The player follows the dark street, finds an older half shoe print and torn cloth, then reaches a fork: left is a narrow blocked lane, right has firelight and burnt-paper smell.
- Turn 13: The player inspects the shoe print and cloth. The evidence points left, but a moving shadow and the right-side firelight remain active.
- Turns 14-15: The player challenges the moving shadow and uses the camera flash. The figure is revealed as the gold-haired girl from the festival.
- Turns 16-20: The girl is identified as Karina, an “暗街的调停人.” The player asks about Mint. Karina implies Mint owes something not payable by money or apology. The burnt-paper smell and right-hand firelight continue as a route cue.

## 21-30 Evaluated

- Turn 21: The player asks what Mint owed. Karina gives an evasive answer: Mint gave the protagonist some things and took others, leaving only debt. She then leaves into the dark. The choices point toward the burnt-paper smell, reflection, or pursuing Karina further.
- Turn 22: The player follows the burnt-paper smell to a burning iron barrel. They find a copper-red cloth fragment, a burned paper fragment with “帕...” remaining, burned fibers, and faint shoe prints continuing deeper into the dark street.
- Turn 23: The player photographs the ash, cloth, paper, footprints, and environment. The deeper drip sound becomes the next route cue.
- Turn 24: The player follows the shoe prints and drip sound into a narrower, darker, wetter passage. The route descends and the player reaches a bend.
- Turn 25: The player stops to listen. They infer a thick metal surface or inverted bell ahead, in a low, enclosed, wet space.
- Turn 26: The player opens the camera bag compartment and tries to use screen light. The text confirms the red cloth and burned paper are still present, then turns the camera screen on. The choices decide what to do with the light.
- Turn 27: The player turns the screen off and advances by touch and sound, eventually reaching a suspended inverted iron bell.
- Turn 28: The player presses against the bell and listens to the changing drip rhythm. They infer the bell is tilted toward the deeper dark street and that water collects and falls from its rim.
- Turn 29: The player traces the water-worn groove down the bell, finds the drip point, the water hollow below it, and another shoe print pointing deeper.
- Turn 30: The player examines the water hollow and shoe print by hand, confirming the footprint owner moved quickly and did not look back.
