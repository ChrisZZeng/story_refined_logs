# Window Timeline 41-60

## Context Turns 41-50

- 41: The player asks why 卡琳娜 would attend the banquet. She explains that as 暗街的申诉人 she must occupy the visible place assigned to 暗街, or others will pay the price.
- 42: The player asks whether she remembers her original self. 卡琳娜 says she has been the申诉人 so long that she can barely separate that role from herself, then asks the player the same question.
- 43: The player calls out that she is deflecting. 卡琳娜 admits the question is hard to answer.
- 44: The player asks how 卡尔 would answer. 卡琳娜 says 卡尔 would say she has never changed, only needs to remember it.
- 45: The player says 卡尔 probably sees her as unchanged. 卡琳娜 agrees and invites the next question.
- 46: The player asks how 卡尔 became the cat who sees through everything. 卡琳娜 says she does not know where 卡尔 came from, but recalls first meeting her seven years ago.
- 47: The player asks what happened after that first meeting. 卡琳娜 recounts 卡尔 sleeping on her lap through the night and saying, “你坐得够稳了.”
- 48: The player asks why 卡尔 chose her. 卡琳娜 says 卡尔 chose what she did, not her name: she saved someone who should not have died.
- 49: The player asks what 卡琳娜 plans after the banquet. She says she may leave 暗街 for a few days to confirm something while she still can.
- 50: The player offers to listen. 卡琳娜 says she may explain later, then asks whether the player found the person he came to 新西西里 to find.

## Evaluated Turns 51-60

- 51: The player deflects 卡琳娜's question and asks what she needs to confirm after leaving 暗街. She asks whether the player is worrying about her.
- 52: The player says he has not found the person he came for. 卡琳娜 asks whether he will continue looking.
- 53: The player says perhaps he came to confirm something rather than find her. 卡琳娜 asks what he wants to confirm.
- 54: The player begins explaining 敏特: he knew her in the war zone, believed she died, then received a photo showing her in 新西西里.
- 55: The player says he owes 敏特 an apology and goodbye. 卡琳娜 observes that he believes she may still be alive and has a chance to say it.
- 56: The player asks whether 卡琳娜 has ever owed someone words. She says yes, but is unsure who the words are for.
- 57: The player asks what she means by “who.” 卡琳娜 says she knows someone who taught her about the city, survival, and what is worth it, but the text uses “他” for that person.
- 58: The player asks when she plans to say the words. 卡琳娜 says timing requires knowing who the words are for; one line contains malformed dialogue quotation.
- 59: The player asks who that person is. 卡琳娜 reveals it is 卡尔 and says “她教会了我很多事.”
- 60: The player asks how 卡尔 taught her. 卡琳娜 explains that 卡尔 did not give answers, but taught her how to ask questions that mattered.
