# Window Timeline: Turns 1-20

1. Paz wakes from a traumatic memory of Mint dying in his arms and finds himself at New Sicily's Resurrection Day festival. Karina returns his journalist ID, and a vendor explains the festival, the island's black-market power structure, and the risk of asking about Mint.
2. Paz asks about Karina. The vendor says Karina runs the festival and has major influence, then redirects Paz toward finding Mint and suggests Antique Street for information.
3. Paz asks for more about the person in the photo. The vendor gives little, hints Karina may know more, warns that information costs money, and ends the conversation.
4. Paz leaves the festival center and reaches Antique Street, a dimmer, quieter area full of old goods, watches, books, silver, and weapon parts.
5. Paz walks deeper through Antique Street, noticing old watches, silver, books, modified firearm parts, wary vendors, and a dirty cat near an unattended parts stall.
6. Paz crouches by the firearm parts stall, examines the modified parts, and notices neighboring vendors watching him indirectly.
7. Paz interacts with the dirty cat. The cat leaves and exposes a note reading “暗街——找‘裁缝’.”
8. Paz goes toward Dark Street, is blocked by three gang members, and is told to leave his camera.
9. Paz pretends to yield by offering the camera, claiming it was a comrade's legacy. The gang hesitates and allows him through.
10. Paz searches Dark Street markings and finds an old wooden door marked with a scissors symbol, matching the tailor clue.
11. Paz pushes the scissors-marked door open and sees a dark tailor-like interior with a worktable, cloth, a treadle sewing machine, and half-finished garments.
12. Paz asks if anyone is there. An elderly woman answers, lets him in, identifies herself as the tailor, and asks whether he has paid for the note.
13. Paz says he can pay and asks the price. The tailor refuses money and demands that Paz retrieve an object from deeper in Dark Street in exchange for information.
14. Paz asks if there is another way. The tailor says there is not and frames the choice as retrieving the object or returning to search for another clue.
15. Paz asks what the object is and why the tailor cannot go herself. She explains the target is in a locked iron box under the floor of an abandoned butcher shop and gives Paz a hand-drawn map.
16. Paz asks about guards and precautions. The tailor describes the butcher shop area, advises that it is best to go before noon while the street is empty, and gives more details about the old lock and hidden floorboard.
17. Paz decides to wait until evening and approach with the festival crowd. The tailor explains how to use the crowd and tells him to return in the evening.
18. Paz hides in a recessed corner near old crates, sits with his camera, studies the map, and waits.
19. Paz rests until the light turns orange and the festival drum band nears Dark Street, then rises and joins the growing crowd.
20. Paz moves with the crowd toward the cracked sign, watches possible gang lookouts, and keeps a natural pace while approaching the butcher shop area.
