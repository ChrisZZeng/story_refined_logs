# Window Timeline, Turns 21-40

## Context Turns 21-30

- Turn 21: Paz slips through a narrow side passage and reaches a position near the butcher shop.
- Turn 22: He checks the side of the butcher shop and finds a rear wooden door, a boarded narrow window, and fresh signs near the steps.
- Turn 23: He returns to the front-facing alley and observes the butcher shop's closed front door from cover.
- Turn 24: He retreats to a hiding place in the main dark alley and sees a broad-shouldered man leave the butcher shop area.
- Turn 25: He follows the man into a narrower alley and hears metal scraping near a side path.
- Turn 26: He approaches and sees the man handling a canvas bag and metal objects near an overturned iron cage.
- Turn 27: He retreats, is surrounded by dark-jacketed men, and Karina intervenes; the men back down. Karina invites him to a safer place to talk.
- Turn 28: Paz asks where Karina is taking him. She says "公寓", then he follows her deeper into the dark street.
- Turn 29: Paz observes the route and landmarks while following Karina. She stops at a gray building and opens a door to stairs.
- Turn 30: Paz asks whether the building is safe. Karina says dark street has no absolute safety, but a place with rules is safer.

## Evaluated Turns 31-40

- Turn 31: Paz asks what rules apply. Karina says not to ask, touch, or say what should not be asked, touched, or said, then goes upstairs.
- Turn 32: Paz calls after her asking if there is anything else. Karina warns him not to touch the blue vase by the window; Paz starts upstairs.
- Turn 33: Paz keeps the vase warning in mind, notices a small wall symbol, and reaches the open apartment doorway. He hears a slight movement inside.
- Turn 34: Paz asks who is inside. Karina says it is her cat Karl and invites him in. Paz enters, sees the room, the blue vase, Karl, and Karina by the fireplace.
- Turn 35: Paz inspects the bookshelf without touching it. Karina permits him to browse, and Karl reacts mildly from the sofa.
- Turn 36: Paz pulls out an untitled thin booklet with a dark red line, but a knocking/door-sound sequence interrupts before he opens it.
- Turn 37: Paz quickly opens the booklet, sees a black-and-white photo and a line of handwriting, then closes it as the knocking escalates.
- Turn 38: Paz asks "谁？" Karina tells him not to speak and moves to the door.
- Turn 39: Paz reaches for his camera while Karina opens the door. A shadowed visitor stands outside holding an unsealed envelope.
- Turn 40: Paz shifts position to see the visitor's face. The visitor remains in shadow and subtly adjusts in response; Karina stays in control of the doorway.
